# ICC Cricket World Cup Summary (2003-2023)

## Winners by Year:
- 2003: Australia (defeated India)
- 2007: Australia (defeated Sri Lanka)
- 2011: India (defeated Sri Lanka)
- 2015: Australia (defeated New Zealand)
- 2019: England (defeated New Zealand)
- 2023: Australia (defeated India)

## Runners-up by Year:
- 2003: India
- 2007: Sri Lanka
- 2011: Sri Lanka
- 2015: New Zealand
- 2019: New Zealand
- 2023: India

## Host Nations:
- 2003: South Africa, Zimbabwe, Kenya
- 2007: West Indies
- 2011: India, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh
- 2015: Australia, New Zealand
- 2019: England
- 2023: India

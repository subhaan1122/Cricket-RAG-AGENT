COMPREHENSIVE CAPTAINCY RECORDS — ALL CAPTAINS (2003-2023)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RICKY PONTING (Australia):
- World Cups as captain: 2003, 2007, 2011
- Total WC matches as captain: 29
- Won: 26, Lost: 3, Win%: 89.7%
- Won World Cup: 2003, 2007 (back-to-back titles)
- 2003: 11 matches, Won 11 (unbeaten — perfect record)
- 2007: 11 matches, Won 11 (unbeaten — perfect record)
- 2011: 7 matches, Won 4, Lost 3 (eliminated in QF by India)
- Only captain to win 2 consecutive World Cups
- Best WC captain by win percentage (min 20 matches): 89.7%

MS DHONI (India):
- World Cups as captain: 2011, 2015, 2019
- Total WC matches as captain: 20
- Won: 14, Lost: 6, Win%: 70.0%
- Won World Cup: 2011
- 2011: 9 matches, Won 7, Lost 2 (won the tournament)
- 2015: 8 matches, Won 5, Lost 3 (lost in semi-final to Australia)
  Actually 2015: India played 8 matches (6 pool + QF + SF). Won 6, Lost 2 (NR 0).
  Lost to Australia in QF? No — India lost in the semi-final to Australia.
  Pool: Won vs Pakistan, SA, UAE, WI, Ireland. Lost to none? Actually India won all 6 pool matches, then beat Bangladesh in QF, lost to Australia in SF.
  2015: 8 matches, Won 7, Lost 1
- 2019: Not captain (Virat Kohli was captain)

VIRAT KOHLI (India):
- World Cups as captain: 2019
- Total WC matches as captain: 9
- Won: 7, Lost: 2, Win%: 77.8%
- 2019: 9 matches (9 league + SF). Won 7, Lost 2. Lost to England (league) and New Zealand (SF).
  India won 7 league matches, lost to England. Then lost in SF to New Zealand.
  Actually: India played 9 league matches, won 7, lost 1, 1 no result. Then SF: lost to NZ.
  Total: 10 matches (including SF), Won 7, Lost 2, NR 1.

EOIN MORGAN (England):
- World Cups as captain: 2015, 2019
- Total WC matches as captain: 17
- Won: 10, Lost: 7, Win%: 58.8%
- 2015: 6 matches, Won 2, Lost 4 (eliminated in group stage — England's worst WC performance)
- 2019: 11 matches, Won 8, Lost 3 (won the World Cup)
- Transformed England cricket between 2015 and 2019
- Won World Cup: 2019

KANE WILLIAMSON (New Zealand):
- World Cups as captain: 2019, 2023
- Total WC matches as captain: 17
- Won: 9, Lost: 8, Win%: 52.9%
- 2019: 10 matches, Won 6, Lost 4 (reached final, lost to England in Super Over)
  NZ won 5 league matches, lost 3, 1 NR. Then beat India in SF. Lost final.
  Total: Won 7, Lost 3. Wait — let me recalculate:
  League: P9, W5, L3, NR1. SF: Won vs India. Final: Lost to England.
  Total: 11 matches, Won 6, Lost 4, NR 1
- 2023: 7 matches, Won 3, Lost 4 (eliminated in group stage... wait NZ reached semis?)
  Actually NZ: P10, W5, L5 in league. Then lost SF to India.
  Total 2023: 10 matches, Won 5, Lost 5 (not captain for all — he was injured for some)
- Runner-up: 2019 (lost in the most dramatic final ever)

ROHIT SHARMA (India):
- World Cups as captain: 2023
- Total WC matches as captain: 11
- Won: 10, Lost: 1, Win%: 90.9%
- 2023: Won all 10 league matches, then won SF vs New Zealand
  Lost the final to Australia
  Total: 11 matches, Won 10, Lost 1
- Highest win% for a WC losing finalist captain

PAT CUMMINS (Australia):
- World Cups as captain: 2023
- Total WC matches as captain: 11
- Won: 9, Lost: 2, Win%: 81.8%
- 2023: Won 7 league matches, lost 2 (to India and South Africa). Won SF vs SA. Won Final vs India.
- Won World Cup: 2023

MICHAEL CLARKE (Australia):
- World Cups as captain: 2015
- Total WC matches as captain: 8
- Won: 7, Lost: 1, Win%: 87.5%
- 2015: Won all pool matches, QF vs Pakistan, SF vs India. Won Final vs New Zealand.
  Lost: 1 match (vs New Zealand in pool stage)
- Won World Cup: 2015
- Clarke scored 74 in the final — his last ODI match before retirement

SOURAV GANGULY (India):
- World Cups as captain: 2003
- Total WC matches as captain: 11
- Won: 9, Lost: 2, Win%: 81.8%
- 2003: Led India to the final (lost to Australia by 125 runs)
- Key performance: 183 vs Sri Lanka (Johannesburg) — highest by a WC captain at the time

GRAEME SMITH (South Africa):

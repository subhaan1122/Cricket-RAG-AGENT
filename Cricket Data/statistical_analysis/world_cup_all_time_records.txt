AB de Villiers' 31-ball century vs West Indies in the 2015 World Cup is the fastest century in World Cup history. He scored 162* off just 66 balls with 17 fours and 8 sixes.

Kevin O'Brien's 50-ball century for Ireland vs England in the 2011 World Cup at Bangalore was the fastest at that time and remains one of the greatest WC upsets. Ireland chased down 327 with O'Brien scoring 113 off 63 balls.

# 🏏 Cricket World Cup Companion

## Welcome to Your Cricket Knowledge Hub

Imagine having a passionate cricket fan who knows everything about the ICC World Cup tournaments from 2003 to 2023. That's exactly what this intelligent cricket companion does - it brings the excitement of cricket history right to your fingertips!

## 🎯 What This Companion Does

### Your Cricket Conversation Partner
- **Ask Anything About Cricket**: Whether you want to know who won the 2019 World Cup, compare player performances, or explore tournament statistics
- **Natural Conversations**: Chat like you're talking to a cricket expert friend - no robotic responses, just engaging cricket talk
- **Deep Cricket Knowledge**: Covers every ICC World Cup from 2003 to 2023, with details about matches, players, teams, and memorable moments

#### 🏆 Tournament Explorer
- Discover winners, runners-up, and final match highlights
- Explore tournament formats and how they evolved over the years
- Learn about hosting countries and their impact on the games

#### 👥 Player Spotlight
- Compare batting and bowling performances across tournaments
- Find out who scored the most runs or took the most wickets
- Explore player achievements and career highlights in World Cups

#### 📊 Match Insights
- Relive exciting matches with detailed summaries
- Understand team strategies and key moments
- Get statistics that bring the action to life

#### 🌍 Global Cricket Stories
- Learn how different teams performed across multiple tournaments
- Discover cricket's evolution through the World Cup lens
- Connect with cricket's rich history and traditions
